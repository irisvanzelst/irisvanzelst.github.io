30 March 2023

Venus in the media

I wrote an article for NRC (NL) on the recent discovery of active volcanism on Venus and the untimely decision of NASA to cut funding for its VERITAS mission to Venus. Because of this article, I was also invited for a live radio interview on De Nieuws BV on NPO Radio 1 (NL). Exciting times!

I submitted my paper on estimating Venus's seismicity to GRL! The preprint can be found on EarthArxiv.

18 March 2023

Volcanism on Venus in Algemeen Dagblad (NL)

After the news broke on the observation of active volcanism on Venus (Herrick & Hensley, Science, 2023), several Dutch news outlets reported on it. I was featured in an article in the Algemeen Dagblad (NL) to comment on the significance of this discovery. You can find the complete article here.
