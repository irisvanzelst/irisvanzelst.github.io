Outreach

Outreach is one of my favourite things to do, because I love to get people enthusiastic about the Earth. I am the Editor-in-Chief of the EGU Geodynamics Blog, for which I write and edit blog posts aimed at the general public and fellow Earth scientists alike. I am also spearheading the successful short course 'Geodynamics 101', which is taught annually at the EGU General Assembly.

I am always open for new outreach opportunities, so please don't hesitate to contact me if you have an idea or would like to collaborate.
